7. Bookings & Payment Terms

Booking Process

A Booking is formed when: (a) a Client submits a Booking request; (b) a Fundi accepts the request; and (c) the Client confirms the Booking. A confirmed Booking constitutes a legally binding agreement between the Client and the Fundi.

Platform Fees

  • FundiConnect charges a service fee of 10% on the total value of each completed Booking, deducted from the Fundi's payout.
  • Clients are charged a booking fee of Ksh 50 per confirmed Booking, non-refundable.
  • Emergency bookings (via Emergency Mode) carry an additional Ksh 100 priority fee.
  • All fees are inclusive of applicable taxes under Kenyan law, including VAT where required.

Off-Platform Transactions — STRICTLY PROHIBITED

🚫 Circumvention Policy: If a Client and Fundi first connect through FundiConnect, any subsequent bookings between those parties must continue to be processed through the Platform for a period of 24 months. Attempting to arrange off-platform transactions to avoid service fees is a material breach of these Terms and may result in immediate account termination and a penalty fee.

Pricing Transparency

  • Fundis must display a minimum hourly rate on their profile.
  • Final pricing must be agreed upon before work commences.
  • Mid-job price changes are only permitted with the Client's written (in-app) consent.
  • FundiConnect reserves the right to remove Fundis who systematically charge significantly above their listed rates.

8. M-Pesa Payments & Escrow System

FundiConnect uses a secure escrow payment system to protect both Clients and Fundis:

How Escrow Works

  1. At booking confirmation, the Client pays the agreed amount via M-Pesa Paybill into FundiConnect's escrow account.
  2. Funds are held securely and are not accessible to the Fundi until job completion is confirmed.
  3. After the job is completed, the Client has 24 hours to confirm completion or raise a dispute in the app.
  4. If no dispute is raised within 24 hours, funds are automatically released to the Fundi, minus FundiConnect's service fee.
  5. In case of a dispute, funds remain frozen until the FundiConnect Dispute Team resolves the matter (see Section 11).

M-Pesa Security

  • Always verify the correct Paybill Number and Account Number before making any payment.
  • FundiConnect will never request payment via personal M-Pesa numbers or informal channels.
  • Keep your M-Pesa transaction confirmation SMS — you will need this reference number for any disputes.

Refunds

  • Refunds for valid cancellations are processed within 3–5 business days back to the original M-Pesa number.
  • Refunds for disputed jobs are processed within 7–14 business days pending investigation outcome.
  • M-Pesa transaction charges are non-refundable.
ℹ️ FundiConnect processes M-Pesa transactions through Safaricom's licensed API. By using our payment service, you also agree to Safaricom's M-Pesa Terms and Conditions.

9. Cancellation & Rescheduling Policy

Client Cancellations

  • More than 24 hours before: Full refund. No charges.
  • 6–24 hours before: 50% refund. The Fundi receives 25% as a cancellation fee.
  • Less than 6 hours before / No-show: No refund. Full payment goes to the Fundi.
  • Emergency bookings: Non-refundable once a Fundi has been dispatched.

Fundi Cancellations

  • If a Fundi cancels more than 24 hours before the scheduled time: Client receives full refund. Fundi receives a warning on their account.
  • If a Fundi cancels less than 6 hours before or is a no-show: Client receives full refund plus a Ksh 200 compensation. Fundi receives a negative mark that may affect their ranking.
  • Repeated cancellations (3+ in any 90-day period) will result in account suspension pending review.

How to Cancel

Cancellations must be made through the FundiConnect app or website under "My Bookings." Phone or WhatsApp cancellations are not accepted for refund eligibility purposes.

11. Dispute Resolution

FundiConnect provides a structured dispute resolution process for conflicts between Clients and Fundis. This process is available for 14 days after job completion.

Grounds for a Dispute

  • Work not completed to the agreed standard or not completed at all.
  • Damage to property caused during the course of the job.
  • Overcharging beyond the agreed price without prior consent.
  • Theft or misappropriation of property.
  • Fundi no-show after payment was made.
  • Client refusing to release escrow funds without valid reason after job is complete.

Dispute Process

  1. File Dispute: Submit a dispute through the app within 14 days of the job, with supporting evidence (photos, messages, receipts).
  2. Notification: Both parties are notified and have 48 hours to provide their account and any additional evidence.
  3. Review: FundiConnect's Dispute Team reviews all evidence, which may take 3–7 business days.
  4. Decision: A binding decision is issued. Funds in escrow are released accordingly.
  5. Appeal: Either party may appeal within 5 days to a Senior Dispute Officer. The appeal decision is final.
⚠️ FundiConnect's dispute decisions are final and binding on platform-mediated payments. For criminal matters (theft, assault, fraud), you should additionally report to the Kenya Police Service at your nearest station.
