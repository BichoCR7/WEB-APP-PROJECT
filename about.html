🎯 Our Mission

Making Trust the Default,
Not the Exception

Every day, Kenyans waste hours chasing unreliable fundis, overpaying, or getting poor-quality work with no recourse. We believe every homeowner deserves access to a skilled, verified professional — and every hardworking fundi deserves a fair platform to grow their business.

FundiConnect creates accountability on both sides: fundis earn verified badges and build real reputations, while clients get escrow-backed payments and guaranteed quality.

💡 Our Values

What We Stand For

🛡️

Trust First

Every fundi is ID-verified before they can take a single booking. No exceptions. No shortcuts.

⚖️

Fair to Both Sides

Clients are protected by escrow. Fundis are protected by fair pay policies. We don't take sides — we build systems that work for everyone.

🚀

Fundi Empowerment

We help skilled tradespeople build real businesses — profiles, reviews, consistent income, and career growth through our ranking system.

⚡

Speed & Simplicity

Book a fundi in under 60 seconds. Our emergency mode connects you to the nearest available professional instantly.

🔒

Payment Security

M-Pesa escrow means your money is safe. You only pay out when you're satisfied with the job. No trust issues, no cash disputes.

🌍

Built for Kenya

M-Pesa payments, Swahili-friendly interface, county-level coverage — we're not a global platform copy-pasted to Kenya. We're Kenyan-built.

📖 Our Story

From Frustration
to Platform

2023 — The Problem
The Idea Sparks

Our founder spent 3 days trying to find a reliable electrician in Nairobi. The fourth one quoted triple the market rate. There had to be a better way.

Early 2024 — Building
Platform Development Begins

A small team of Kenyan developers and designers build the first version of FundiConnect — starting with verification, search, and M-Pesa integration.

Mid 2024 — Launch
Beta Launch in Nairobi

50 verified fundis, 200 early clients. First booking made within 4 hours of going live. First 5-star review received that same evening.

2025 — Growth
Going National

Expanding to Mombasa, Kisumu, Eldoret and 15 more counties. 384 verified fundis. Over 1,200 jobs successfully completed across Kenya.
