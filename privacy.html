Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy describes how FundiConnect Kenya ("we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and protects your personal information when you use our platform.

TL;DR: We collect only what we need to run the platform, we never sell your data, and you can request deletion of your data at any time.

1. 📌 Overview

FundiConnect operates as a two-sided marketplace connecting clients with skilled tradespeople ("fundis") across Kenya. Our platform involves sharing some personal data to make bookings, payments, and communication work. We take your privacy seriously and are committed to transparency.

By creating an account or using our services, you agree to this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree, please do not use our platform.

2. 📋 Data We Collect

We collect the following categories of information:

Account Information

  • Full name, phone number, email address
  • Profile photo (for fundis and optionally for clients)
  • Physical location / service area (county and sub-county)
  • Role (Client or Fundi)

Fundi Verification Data

  • National ID or Passport number and image
  • Skills, certifications, and portfolio photos
  • Years of experience

Usage & Transaction Data

  • Booking history — dates, services, amounts, statuses
  • M-Pesa transaction references
  • Chat messages exchanged between client and fundi
  • Ratings and reviews submitted
  • Device information (browser type, OS) and IP address

3. 🎯 How We Use Your Data

We use your data strictly for:

  • Creating and managing your account
  • Matching clients with suitable fundis based on location, skills, and availability
  • Processing M-Pesa payments and managing escrow
  • Sending SMS/email notifications about bookings
  • Verifying fundi identities and preventing fraud
  • Resolving disputes between clients and fundis
  • Improving our platform based on usage analytics
  • Sending platform updates and promotional offers (you can opt out any time)
We will NEVER: sell your personal data to third parties, use your data for purposes unrelated to our platform, or share your M-Pesa PIN (we never see it — payments go through Safaricom's secure API).

4. 🤝 Data Sharing

Your data may be shared with:

  • Fundis you book: Your name, phone number, location, and job description are shared with the fundi you've hired, so they can contact you and find your address.
  • Clients who book you: Fundis' names, phone numbers, service areas, and profile photos are visible to clients.
  • Safaricom M-Pesa: Transaction amounts and phone numbers are processed through the M-Pesa API. We do not store or see your M-Pesa PIN.
  • Legal authorities: If required by Kenyan law (e.g., court order or police investigation), we may disclose your data.

We do not share your data with advertisers, data brokers, or any third parties for commercial purposes.

5. 💳 M-Pesa & Payment Security

Payment processing on FundiConnect is handled through Safaricom's official M-Pesa API (Daraja). Here's how it works:

  • You initiate an M-Pesa payment from your phone — a Safaricom prompt appears on your device
  • You enter your PIN directly into Safaricom's secure system — we never see your PIN
  • Safaricom confirms the transaction and sends us a reference code
  • We hold the confirmed amount in escrow until the job is complete
  • On completion, we initiate a B2C payout to the fundi's registered phone number

All payment data is encrypted using HTTPS/TLS during transmission.

6. 🗓️ Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active. If you delete your account:

  • Profile data is deleted within 30 days
  • Chat messages are deleted within 30 days
  • Transaction records are retained for 7 years as required by Kenyan financial regulations
  • Fundi verification documents are deleted within 90 days of account deletion

7. ✅ Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access — Request a copy of all personal data we hold about you
  • Correction — Update or correct inaccurate information in your profile
  • Deletion — Request deletion of your account and personal data (subject to legal retention requirements)
  • Opt-out — Unsubscribe from marketing communications at any time via your account settings
  • Portability — Request your booking and review history in a machine-readable format

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at privacy@fundiconnect.ke or WhatsApp +254 700 000 000.

8. 🍪 Cookies

We use minimal cookies and browser localStorage to:

  • Keep you logged in (session cookies)
  • Remember your role preference (client/fundi) on the login page
  • Store anonymous analytics data to improve user experience

We do not use advertising cookies or third-party tracking pixels. You can clear cookies at any time via your browser settings — this will log you out of your account.

9. 👶 Children's Privacy

FundiConnect is not intended for users under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect data from minors. If you believe a child has created an account on our platform, please contact us immediately and we will delete the account promptly.

10. 🔄 Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy occasionally to reflect changes in our practices or Kenyan law. When we make significant changes, we will:

  • Update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page
  • Send an SMS notification to all registered users
  • Display a banner in the app for 7 days

Continued use of FundiConnect after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
