What is FundiConnect?⌄
FundiConnect is Kenya's trusted marketplace for booking verified fundis (skilled tradespeople) — including electricians, plumbers, carpenters, painters, and more. We connect clients to pre-verified professionals using M-Pesa escrow payments, real ratings, and smart matching.
How do I create an account?⌄
Click "Get Started" and choose whether you're signing up as a Client (to hire fundis) or a Fundi (to offer services). Fill in your name, phone number, email, and location. You'll receive an OTP on your phone to verify your number. Done in under 2 minutes!
Is FundiConnect free to use for clients?⌄
Yes! Browsing profiles, searching for fundis, and creating bookings are completely free for clients. You only pay the agreed service fee when you book a fundi, and that payment is held securely in escrow until the job is done.
Which counties is FundiConnect available in?⌄
We currently cover 18 counties including Nairobi, Mombasa, Kisumu, Eldoret, Thika, Nakuru, Machakos, Nyeri, Meru, Kakamega, and more. We're expanding rapidly — if your county isn't listed, register your interest and we'll prioritize your area.
How do I book a fundi?⌄
Search for a fundi by service or location → View their profile and reviews → Click "Book Now" → Choose your date and time → Describe the job → Submit. The fundi receives your request instantly and will Accept or Decline within a few hours.
Can I cancel a booking?⌄
Yes. You can cancel a booking from your dashboard before the fundi arrives. If you cancel more than 2 hours before the scheduled time, you receive a full refund. Cancellations within 2 hours may incur a small cancellation fee (Ksh 200) to compensate the fundi for their time.
What is Emergency Booking?⌄
Emergency Booking lets you instantly connect to the nearest available fundi for urgent situations like burst pipes, power outages, or lockouts. The system automatically finds and connects you to the closest fundi — no need to browse profiles or fill in dates.
What if the fundi doesn't show up?⌄
If a fundi doesn't show up, mark the booking as "No Show" in your dashboard. You'll receive a full automatic refund, and our team will investigate and penalize the fundi's account. Repeat no-shows result in permanent removal from the platform.
How does escrow payment work?⌄
When you book a fundi, you pay via M-Pesa upfront. FundiConnect holds that money securely in escrow. The fundi only receives payment after you confirm the job is complete and you're satisfied. This protects both parties — you don't lose money if the job isn't done, and the fundi is guaranteed payment once it is.
Can I pay cash instead of M-Pesa?⌄
Yes, cash payments are supported. However, we strongly recommend M-Pesa escrow for your protection. Cash bookings are not covered by our dispute resolution system, meaning if there's a problem, we have limited ability to help retrieve your money.
How do I request a refund?⌄
Go to your Dashboard → Bookings → Find the booking → Click "Raise Dispute" or "Request Refund." Our team reviews the case within 24–48 hours. Approved refunds are sent back to the original M-Pesa number within 2–5 business days.
What are the platform fees?⌄
Clients pay no platform fees. Fundis pay a 10% commission on each completed job. For example, if a job is Ksh 3,000, the fundi earns Ksh 2,700 net. This is how we fund the platform, verification systems, and support team.
How do I register as a fundi?⌄
Click "Join as Fundi" and create your account. Upload your National ID, a clear profile photo, your skills, and optionally certificates and portfolio photos. Our admin team reviews your application within 24–72 hours. Once approved, you can start receiving bookings immediately.
When do I get paid?⌄
Once a client marks a job as "Complete," your payment is released from escrow immediately to your M-Pesa number. The money typically arrives within minutes. You can also view your earnings history and pending payouts in your Fundi Dashboard.
How do I get more bookings?⌄
Complete your profile 100% — add a clear photo, all your skills, a bio, and portfolio photos. Respond to booking requests quickly (fast response is ranked in our algorithm). Deliver quality work and collect 5-star reviews. Fundis with higher ratings appear first in search results.
What is the ranking system (Bronze/Silver/Gold/Elite)?⌄
Our ranking system rewards the best fundis: Bronze (0–20 jobs), Silver (21–50 jobs, avg 4.0+ rating), Gold (51–100 jobs, avg 4.5+), Elite (100+ jobs, avg 4.7+). Higher ranks come with better placement in search results, Elite badge on profile, and priority customer support.
How does FundiConnect verify fundis?⌄
Every fundi must submit a valid Kenyan National ID or Passport. Our admin team manually verifies the document and cross-references it with the profile photo before approving the account. Only then can a fundi receive bookings. Clients can always see a fundi's verified badge on their profile.
What if I feel unsafe during a booking?⌄
Your safety is our top priority. If at any point during a booking you feel unsafe: end the session, move to a safe location, and call the police if necessary (999). Then report the incident through your dashboard. The fundi's account will be immediately suspended pending investigation. You will not be charged for the booking.
How are disputes resolved?⌄
Raise a dispute through your dashboard. Our team reviews the chat history, job description, photos, and both parties' statements. We aim to resolve disputes within 48 hours. If the dispute is in your favour, the escrow funds are released back to you. We have resolved 94% of disputes successfully.
